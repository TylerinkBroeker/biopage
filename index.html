However, as with my hobbies, I am excited to continue learning and am looking forward to becoming adept at many more languages and technologies.

In retail, I have earned the confidence of my managers and peers for being trustworthy, accountable, competent, kind, and hard working.

I am looking forward to finding a new career in the field, and hope to become part of a team that values the work ethic, enthusiasm, and skills I have to offer.
